# 03 — Explain global changes and degraded runs

**What to build:** Keep reports concise and trustworthy when most of a site changed or when enrichment is unavailable.

**Blocked by:** 01 — Generate an understandable report from evidence.

**Status:** ready-for-agent

- [ ] Missing captions or AI data produces a useful deterministic explanation.
- [ ] Infrastructure errors are separated from problems caused by the change.
